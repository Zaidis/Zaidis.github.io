Arduino Keyboard Project
This was my final project for my class Computer Organizations and Systems where I programmed my arduino to play and record musical notes. It uses five different buttons that each hold two functions/modes. I can switch between the nodes using a potentiometer. There are eight different notes that can be played, and the other two buttons hold the functionality of resetting the song and playing the song back to you.

Our task was to simply create our own project that uses a few tools from our arduino kits. Since I love music, I decided this would be a fun and interesting project to showcase for my class. I also made a video demonstration.

Software Used: Arduino
Languages: C++
Status: Complete
