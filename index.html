About Me


Hello! My name is Adam Weber. I am a passionate Game Programmer and Game Designer who has been making and developing games ever since the year 2018! I am a graduate from University of Wisconsin - Whitewater who obtained a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Media Arts and Game Development - Communication/Gaming. I have made the deans list in each semester that I attended there and graduated magna cum laude .

Before I went to college, I was a part of a F.I.R.S.T. FRC team, CORE 2062. In my time there, I was the lead designer of its website and worked closely with sponsors. Many of my changes and designs are still on the website today, and I am proud to be an alumni for such an amazing organization. You can see the website here!

Throughout my time in college, I participated in as many opportunities as I could to better my skills and gain more connections. I worked in research projects alongside several professors, a study abroad project (became remote due to Covid 19), a few independent study courses, personal and team-oriented projects, and dozens and dozens of game jams! It goes without saying that I love game jams. Many of these projects are found here on my portfolio below or on my itch page!

Today, I am proud to say that I now have roughly 1.7 years of work experience under my belt through Filament Games as a Game Engineer where I worked in an agile environment with UX/UI designers, game designers, producers, web developers, QA, and of course other game engineers. To see some of my work, visit my Professional Work page here on my portfolio!

Outside of my work experience, I co-own a small game studio with a couple friends from college that is currently developing board games! Independently from that, I also am working on a deck-building tower defense that I plan to publish on Steam this year. Feel free to ask me anything about these projects!
