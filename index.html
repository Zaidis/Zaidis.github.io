My name is Adam Weber. I am a passionate Game Programmer and Game Designer, and I specialize in Front End programming and Level Design.

About Me


I am a senior at University of Wisconsin - Whitewater striving for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Media Arts and Game Development - Communication/Gaming. I have made the deans list in every semester so far, and I intend to keep that streak to the end.

Before I went to college, I was a part of a F.I.R.S.T. FRC team called CORE Robotics. In my time there, I was the lead designer of its website and worked closely with sponsors. Many of my changes and designs are still on the website today, and I am proud to be an alumni for such an amazing organization. You can see the website here!

In my three years in Whitewater thus far, I have worked hard to learn and gain as much experience as possible in the gaming field. I have been a part of a study abroad project (became remote due to Covid 19), personal and team based projects, and numerous game jams during the school years and summers.

I push myself to become better everyday. Making games is my passion, and I hope one day that I can be a part of a great team working for the same goal: to create amazing games that will be cherished by many.
